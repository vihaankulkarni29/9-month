Our First Valentine's Day

The Power of Three Little Words

"Vihaan, I want to tell you something", as we were walking past Jai Hind college, when I thought you were going to say I love you but you didn't. We both looked at each other and smiled and both of us knew what the other felt. I remember being in the rickshaw from Goregaon station where you said, "Vihaan, I love you", which are still the most important words I heard from you and I'll always cherish that. Eating momos after that and we could not stop smiling at all.

What Made It Special:

  • The moment you finally said "I love you"
  • The rickshaw ride from Goregaon station
  • Eating momos with endless smiles
  • Knowing our love was mutual and deep

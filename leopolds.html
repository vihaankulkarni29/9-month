The magical moment when I asked you to be my girlfriend

Leopold's Cafe, Colaba

The Beginning of Our Beautiful Story

That evening at Leopold's was magical. The bustling atmosphere of Colaba, the aroma of freshly brewed coffee, and the sound of waves crashing nearby created the perfect backdrop for what would become the start of our incredible journey together.

I remember how nervous I was, my heart pounding as I looked into your eyes and asked you to be my girlfriend. The moment you said yes, the world seemed to light up. It was the beginning of something truly special.

Leopold's will always hold a special place in our hearts - the place where "we" officially began. Every time we pass by Colaba, we smile knowing this iconic cafe witnessed the start of our love story.

What I Remember Most:

  • The way you smiled when I asked you
  • The perfect lighting in the cafe
  • How natural everything felt between us
  • The excitement of our future together
